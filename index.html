About Us

Smart Guys Men's & Women's Beauty Salon

Welcome to Smart Guys Men's & Women's Beauty Salon, your neighbourhood destination for top-tier grooming and beauty services in Mugalivakkam, Chennai. Nestled on Kundrathur Main Road in Lawrence Nagar, we have built a reputation for exceptional haircuts, styling, and beauty treatments delivered by skilled and passionate professionals. With a stellar 4.9-star rating from over 334 happy customers, we take pride in every service we offer.

At Smart Guys Salon, we believe that great grooming is more than just a service — it is a confidence booster. Whether you are stepping in for a quick trim, a birthday special haircut, or a complete makeover, our friendly staff ensures you leave looking and feeling your absolute best. Open every day from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, we make premium salon care accessible and convenient for the entire family.
